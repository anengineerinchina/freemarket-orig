Welcome to NXT FreeMarket, the global, private marketplace based on the NXT blockchain! NXT FreeMarket is under active development, so please watch for new versions.

There are no FreeMarket fees for the rest of 2014!

Visit us on the web at: NXTFreeMarket.com.

Getting Started

To get started, use the menu above.

The Buy menu lets you search for items to buy and view your completed purchases. The Sell menu lets you list a new item for sale, and view all the items you have listed.

In order to make a purchase or list a new item, you must be logged in. Use the secret phrase associated with your NXT account to log in.
